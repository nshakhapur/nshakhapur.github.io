Experiences

Machine Learning Intern @ National Centre for Biological Sciences

I am developing techniques for preprocessing, clustering, visualization, and exploration of single-cell RNA sequencing data using Machine Learning techniques (primarily using KNN Clustering and PCA Models). This will help in identifying the unique patterns between the different microglial cells and track the gene expressions of the proliferating cells. I have worked in collaboration with Krishnaswamy Lab at Yale university.

Artificial Intelligence Intern @Indian Space Research Organasation

As an Artificial Intelligence Intern, I developed the architecture and programmed an advanced Artificial Intelligence model for satellite health anomaly detection, that can be implemented to predict the anomaly in future, thereby allowing the ground control to have time take the necessary actions to prevent the anomaly from occurring. I had an opportunity to work alongside the scientists for NISAR Mission (NASA-ISRO SAR Mission) at UR Rao Satellite Centre, ISRO.
